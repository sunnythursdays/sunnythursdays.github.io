Times have changed, nothing stays the same… but I never forgot the poems I’ve made!

This EP revisits a collection of early works originally released under a different alias. Created primarily in Bosca Ceoil, these tracks reflect an important & formative period of experimentation, built with limited tools & a range of voicebanks. They now reappear in a new form. Remastered, and in some cases, remade. The original ideas are preserved, while providing an opportunity for these pieces to be heard as they were always meant to be.
