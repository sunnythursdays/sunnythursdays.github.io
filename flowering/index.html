<Flowering>

SunnyThursdays’ debut mixtape, <Flowering> will be released in 2026. Made as her final gesture before stepping out of her teenage years, the project is all about growing up. She threads together the sounds that shaped her early life: glossy & saccharine pop hooks, snappy vocal chops, nostalgic synth bells, and the faint but persistent hum of existential unease beneath it all.
