SunnyThursdays

Introduction

SunnyThursdays is a one-woman passion project run by a vocal synth music producer, songwriter, digital artist, & full-time uni student. you can call me sunny, thursday, or my real name if we happen to know each other like that. somehow, my friends started calling me “miss thursdays,” which is honestly kinda adorable.

i’m just someone with a growing need to create things that feel beautiful & the most true to myself. most of the time, that becomes music or art, & i share it whenever the timing feels right. (that means i have a habit of shadow dropping releases... but i do announce big stuff!!)

Frequently Asked Questions

ok so initially, i talked about this in an introductory post on the first iteration of the website, but i'll just put it here + expand on it a bit more!

SunnyThursdays is the name i chose to carry forward after a whole bunch of restarts. it carries my personal philosophy + promise to make things gently again. to create not as a mirror of my past, but as a way of moving toward something brighter.

i was born on a sunny thursday! and back in the day when i was growing up, thursdays were generally happy days. we'd spend thursdays planning for weekend trips + i'd stay up during thursday nights on the family pc, ripping off vocaloid music from youtube to transfer to the walkman. yeah, mind you i was like, 6 back then.

that's all for the past! and here i am now. starting anew. i want to write songs that feel like morning light after rain + breathe life into every little thing that reminds me i’m still here. SunnyThursdays felt like a natural choice for a name.

been writing my own songs since 2019, but i only took music production seriously in 2021. the word "seriously" is a stretch though since i started out making crack mashups LOL

SunnyThursdays has been a thing since December 4, 2025. fun fact: i share the same debut date with SF-A2 miki ♥ (and hiyama kiyoteru + kaai yuki buuut i debuted with miki sooo...)

  • Cakewalk Next + Sonar is the my main DAW combo
  • i just use the stock Cakewalk plugins/instruments + Vital
  • sometimes i use Bosca Ceoil + KiraStudio to produce simple demos/ideas for songs + make some classic chiptune/VGM sounds
  • lastly for the vocal synth stuff, i mainly use Synthesizer V Studio + OpenUTAU
